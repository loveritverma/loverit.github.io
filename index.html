Our Story

A Tale of Love, Destiny, and New Beginnings in the Heart of Kashi

  • First We Meet

    March 10th, 2024

    Our story began in one of the most magical places on earth, sacred land of Lord Shiva, Varanasi - India, where tradition and destiny brought us together on a day blessed with divine timing, very next day of Shivratri festival. The energy of the city, filled with devotion and spiritual beauty, surrounded us as we shared our first conversation. Varanasi, with its deep connection to Lord Shiva, will always hold a special place in our hearts, as it marked the start of our journey together.

  • Engagement

    July 12th, 2024

    Our engagement was a beautiful celebration of love, family, and new beginnings. Surrounded by our closest family and friends, we took the first official step toward our future together. It was a day filled with joy, laughter, and blessings as we exchanged rings and promises to build a life filled with love, trust, and togetherness.

  • Towards A Relationship

    Post July 12th, 2024

    After our engagement, the time leading up to our wedding became a beautiful phase of discovery and bonding. As we prepared for our big day, we grew closer with each conversation, sharing our hopes, dreams, and laughter along the way. The excitement of planning our future together, while supported by our families, made every moment special. This time has strengthened our bond, and now, as we approach our wedding day, we look forward to starting the next chapter of our lives as partners for life.
